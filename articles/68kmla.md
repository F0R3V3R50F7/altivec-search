## 68KMLA: Your Classic Mac Community

The 68K Macintosh Liberation Army (68KMLA) is the premier forum for classic Macintosh users. Active members share repair tips, software recommendations, and hardware advice.

## What You'll Find

Sections for every era: Quadra, PowerPC, iBook, PowerMac G4, G5, and beyond. Real expertise, no gatekeeping.

## Getting Started

Visit [68kmla.org](https://68kmla.org), browse, and join the conversation. Ask questions — the community loves helping.
