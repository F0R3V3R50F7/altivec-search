## FrogFind - Search for Old Browsers

A search engine designed from the ground up for retro browsers. It returns lightweight, text-rich results that load instantly on any connection.

## Why Use It

Google and modern search break on old browsers. FrogFind strips the bloat and gives you pure search results: title, snippet, URL.

## Searching

Visit [frogfind.com](https://frogfind.com), type your query, and get instant results without JavaScript or tracking.
