## Why Internet Radio on a G4?

The PowerMac G4 is a perfect jukebox for streaming audio. iTunes 9 and earlier have native support for .m3u and .pls streams. Here's how to set it up.

## Step 1: Download a Stream

You don't have to do anything fancy or use a secondary machine. Just open internet-radio.com directly in Safari on your G4 and find a station you like. Click the .m3u button and it will drop the playlist file straight into your downloads folder.

## Step 2: Open in iTunes

Double-click the newly downloaded .m3u file. iTunes will launch and start playing the stream immediately. You can drag the stream into your Library or a custom playlist for easy access later.

## Recommended Stations

- Ambient Sleeping Pill - 64k stream, perfect for coding
- Radio Paradise - eclectic mix, high quality but G4 handles it
- SomaFM - Groove Salad, classic

## Troubleshooting

If a stream won't load, try lowering the buffer size in iTunes preferences. Some streams require QuickTime 7.
