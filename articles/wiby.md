## Wiby - Discovery Engine for the Indie Web

Wiby searches the web for websites with character: small, independent, and hand-coded. No corporate bloat, no algorithms.

## What Makes It Special

Emphasis on visual design, personality, and substance. You'll find personal blogs, hobby sites, art projects, and forgotten corners of the web.

Visit [wiby.me](https://wiby.me), search for interests, and fall down wonderful rabbit holes.
