## Archive.org - Preserving the Web

The Internet Archive preserves snapshots of websites going back to 1996. Travel back in time and see how your favorite sites looked.

## Search the Wayback Machine

Enter any URL and browse year-by-year snapshots. Find old software download pages, archived forums, and forgotten web design.

## Getting Started

Visit [web.archive.org](https://web.archive.org), type a URL, and click through the calendar to explore history.
